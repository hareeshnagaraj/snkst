## Structure map

1. **Hero** (Ludo full-bleed + Align typography scale) — rock pose, “Yoga with sun in it.”
2. **Intro** (Ludo “Hi I’m…”) — personal voice + research credibility  
3. **Word banner** (Align “STRONGER CALMER…”) — STRONGER · SOFTER · SUNLIT  
4. **Offerings grid** (Align plan cards × Ludo class cards) — 6 offerings from IG  
5. **Kalkan split** — place as character  
6. **Lifestyle gallery** — IG grid energy  
7. **Philosophy + paper** — unique differentiator  
8. **Book** — WhatsApp / phone / form  

## Creative license

- English-first site (IG is bilingual TR/EN)
- Cohesive copy rewritten for web, grounded in IG captions
- No fabricated prices (omitted TL amounts from kids flyer)
- Forms are UI-only (no backend)
- Hero / lifestyle: cropped from her Instagram screenshots
- Prenatal / partner / kids / online cards + Kalkan place photography: high-quality complementary stock (Unsplash) where IG crops still had UI chrome
